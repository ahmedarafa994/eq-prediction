<objective>
Fix training data gain distribution to be truly uniform across the full range.

Purpose: The beta(2,2) distribution concentrates 49% of gain samples in the center third of the magnitude range, starving the model of extreme gain examples. HP/LP filters are even worse -- they sample from [-1, 1] dB instead of the full [-24, 24] dB range. These data biases prevent the model from learning the full gain spectrum.

Output: dataset.py with uniform gain sampling and fixed HP/LP gain ranges. All precomputed caches deleted.
</objective>
